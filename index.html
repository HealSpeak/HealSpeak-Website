About HealSpeak

HealSpeak is an open-source virtual triage system designed to provide healthcare assistance and support to hospitals free of cost. Our mission is for our software to free up hospital staff's time and improve every patients experience when they walk into the hospital for treatment.
Our software will act like a virtual nurse and will not only allow physical nurses to spend their time on more pressing issues, but also break down linguistic barriers via our many features. Join us and in making healthcare a better experience for everyone.

Features

  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Multilingual healthcare support
  • Quick medical assessment
  • Improves the hospital experience
  • Free up staff time
  • Currently supports Child/Young adult Vaccination
  • Open-source and free of cost
